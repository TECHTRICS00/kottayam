Famous Places in Kottayam

Illaveezhapoonchira

A breathtaking hill station with rolling meadows, pristine landscapes, and spectacular panoramic views. Perfect for trekking enthusiasts and nature photographers seeking untouched beauty.

📍 30 km from Kottayam
🌡️ Cool climate year-round
⭐ Best sunrise/sunset views
🥾 Ideal for trekking
📍 Go to Location

Vagamon

A serene hill station blessed with lush green meadows, misty valleys, and sprawling tea plantations. Known for its pleasant climate and scenic beauty throughout the year.

📍 35 km from Kottayam
🌿 Tea gardens & meadows
🏕️ Camping adventures
❄️ Pleasant weather
📍 Go to Location

Poonjar Palace

A magnificent 16th-century palace showcasing traditional Kerala architecture, royal artifacts, and antique collections that tell the story of the Poonjar dynasty's rich heritage.

📍 25 km from Kottayam
🏛️ 16th-century architecture
🎨 Royal artifacts
⏰ Open 9 AM - 5 PM
📍 Go to Location

Illikal Kallu

A magnificent rock formation offering spectacular views of the Western Ghats. This natural wonder is popular among adventure enthusiasts and rock climbing aficionados.

📍 40 km from Kottayam
🧗 Rock climbing spot
🌄 Western Ghats views
🥾 Adventure trekking
📍 Go to Location

Kumarakom Backwaters

A mesmerizing network of waterways, coconut groves, and emerald paddy fields. Famous for luxury houseboat cruises and the renowned Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary.

📍 15 km from Kottayam
🚢 Houseboat cruises
🦅 Bird sanctuary
🐟 Fresh seafood
📍 Go to Location

Marmala Waterfall

A pristine waterfall cascading through dense tropical forests and rocky terrain. Perfect for nature lovers seeking tranquility and natural swimming pools.

📍 45 km from Kottayam
🌊 Natural swimming
🌳 Dense forest setting
🥾 Short trek required
📍 Go to Location
